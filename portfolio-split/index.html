About

Building the web,
one component at a time

A Full Stack Developer who does the entire spectrum of building for the web, from wiring up a clean API to crafting the pixel-level details of a UI that actually feels good to use. Most of my time lives on the front end and the systems that power it: JavaScript, React, Node, and the problem-solving that connects them.

I am currently a student, which means I am building constantly, not to ship products yet, but to understand them deeply. Every project is a deliberate attempt to close a gap: a concept I have not implemented, a tool I have not used, a problem I have not solved from scratch without a tutorial.

Alongside the full stack work, I have started dipping into Generative AI , using machine learning to make applications smarter and more responsive to real user needs. It is a small but genuinely exciting part of my toolkit, and I am curious to see where it takes the things I build.
